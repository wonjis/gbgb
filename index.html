About UMich Events

UMich Events is a student-built platform designed to solve the problem of fragmented event information across campus. We automatically scrape and aggregate events from 25+ official university sources, making it easy for students to discover opportunities relevant to their interests and goals.

Our mission is to increase student engagement, help organizers reach wider audiences, and ensure no student misses out on valuable experiences due to information overload.
